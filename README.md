AN AI News Aggregator with an e-ink theme (can be swapped).

Supports Hacker News, Reddit, ArXiv, Github, Mastodon and RSS.

Comments from Hacker News and Reddit are supported.  

Duplicate entries are de-duped and non-english items removed.

Accessible at: https://jimliddle.github.io/ai-news-aggregator/
